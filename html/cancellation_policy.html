CANCELLATION POLICY

Hello! First and foremost, ALL CANCELLATIONS AND RESCHEDULING MUST BE GIVEN AT LEAST 24 HOURS IN ADVANCE! Failure to cancel or reschedule before the 24-hour period will result in an ADDITIONAL FEE based on the package. All Cancellations will result in PARTIAL REFUND which would include everything EXCEPT INITIAL DEPOSIT! Customers will receive their photos within a 72-hour Time frame after the shoot. Failure to deliver these photos to the customer within this given time frame will result in an automatic reimbursement of the initial deposit to the Customer. If we don't reach out within 5 Business Days after the date of your event with your pictures you will be eligible for a FULL REFUND along with Photos taken at the shoot.
